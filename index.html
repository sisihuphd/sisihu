Hello, I'm Sisi Hu!

I am a Ph.D. from the Missouri School of Journalism. I will join the School of Journalism and Strategic Media at the University of Arkansas as an assistant professor this coming fall. My research is rooted in strategic communication with an emphasis on health and science communication.

My teaching specializations are strategic communication research and practice. I have five years of professional work experience in advertising and magazine fields that have heavily influenced my hands-on approach to teaching.
